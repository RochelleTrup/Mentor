MY JOURNEY

Balancing my personal life with my professional commitments has always been a challenging endeavor.

As a woman, there were countless moments when significant events and
priorities seemed to slip through the cracks.

Two decades ago, I committed to a transformative practice: an annual reflection on the year gone by
and the changes I aspired to make in the coming year.

My credentials

MY APPROACH

For 20 years, a friend and I established a ritual where annually, we'd
dedicate two days to discuss work-life balance questions.

Through this, we held each other accountable, challenging and mentoring
each other through our answers.

The strength of this ritual lay not only in the questions but in our mutual accountability.
Being challenged and scrutinized promoted real introspection and growth.

PERSPECTIVE

From my experience as a female founder and in C-suite roles, I've felt the tension between
professional demands and personal well-being.

A mentor, holding you accountable in both business and personal aspects, is invaluable.

They not only broaden your viewpoint but also help pinpoint areas for change, be it professional,
personal, or both for the upcoming year.

MY ASPIRATIONS
