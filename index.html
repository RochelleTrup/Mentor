MY JOURNEY

Balancing my personal life with my professional commitments has always been a challenging endeavor.
As a woman, there were countless moments when significant events and priorities
seemed to slip through the cracks.

Two decades ago, I committed to a transformative practice: an annual reflection on the year gone by
and the changes I aspired to make in the coming year.

My credentials

MY APPROACH

For two decades, a friend and I cultivated a unique ritual. Every year, we'd set aside two days to delve into a series
of carefully refined questions about work-life balance. Through this process, we held each other accountable,
challenging and mentoring one another as we navigated our responses

The true power of this ritual wasn't just in the questions themselves but in our commitment to holding each other accountable.
Being challenged and having our answers scrutinized ensured genuine introspection and growth. This accountability
and mutual mentorship were pivotal, turning our annual tradition into a cornerstone of our personal
and professional success.

PERSPECTIVE

With my experiences as a female founder and the various C-suite roles I've held, I've deeply felt the interplay between professional demands
and personal well-being, and the strain it can place on peace of mind and relationships.

Having a mentor to whom you're accountable, both in a business sense and on a personal level, is invaluable.A mentor who challenges
your responses not only enriches your perspective but also guides you in identifying key areas for change whether
they're purely professional shifts, personal adjustments, or a blend of both for the upcoming year.

"The way to get started is to quit talking and begin doing" - Walt Disney

MY ASPIRATIONS

There is no cost to partake in this initiative. Under the banner of "My Aspirations", my vision extends beyond just a program; it's about laying
the groundwork for a foundation dedicated to assisting female entrepreneurs from underprivileged
backgrounds in the UK.

After thriving in this initiative, I hope to see you become a mentor, providing vital tools and insights to women on their entrepreneurial journey.
Your mentorship can make a significant difference, guiding them toward success in their endeavors

But the aspirations don't end there. In the grander scheme, I envision an investment fund managed and steered by our very own
mentees.This ensures not just the sustainability of our cause but its continued evolution.

As I look ahead, women-led corporations are joining us. Through sponsorships, they contribute,directing proceeds
to our charity. This partnership boosts our ability to support aspiring entrepreneurs.
