My Story

For three decades, I've entwined my business and personal lives, coming to recognize that leading a company as a woman adds layers of complexity and nuance. It isn't just about business success; it's about personal transformation amidst the multiple hats we, as women, wear. I have learnt to understand my passions, values together with limitations and with that knowledge I have been been able to play to my strengths.

Our Reflective Journey

Each year, a close friend and I set aside two days for profound introspection. We explore the challenges of balancing personal relationships with professional demands. we engage in rigorous debates, challenging each other to uncover insights into our business and personal decisions, how each affects the other, and the changes we needed to make. These unique challenges prompted me to reflect annually, evaluating my choices and setting goals for the year ahead.

Perspective

Leading a business isn't just about strategizing and executing. For women, it often intertwines with our roles as mothers, partners, caregivers, and more. This journey is uniquely yours, I bring to the table my experience of navigating this intricate dance. Whether you're facing a business challenge, a relationship hurdle, or the delicate balance of both, I'm here to question and help you find clarity and direction whilst helping establish your goals and the yardsticks by which these goals can be measured.

My Aspiration

I am ardently committed to elevating the next wave of female leaders, especially those who have faced unique challenges. After our mentorship, my hope is that you'll extend your insights, mentoring others in turn. Additionally, I'm establishing a fund to further support budding female entrepreneurs, aiding them in turning their visions into tangible realities.

Commit to Transformation

Our collaborative journey will unfold over four sessions, each 90 minutes long. We'll dive deep into your aspirations, ensuring they're not just dreams but tangible, measurable goals. I'm here to foster reflection, offer my perspective as a woman who's been there, and help you navigate both personal and professional crossroads. And this commitment isn't fleeting. Annually, we'll revisit, assess, and fine-tune, ensuring your path remains authentic to your multifaceted roles and aspirations.
