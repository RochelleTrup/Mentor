My Story

For two decades, I've entwined my business and personal lives, coming to recognize that leading a company as a woman adds layers of complexity and nuance. Beyond the demands of the enterprise, I've navigated the roles of a mother and wife. It isn't just about business success; it's about personal transformation amidst the multiple hats we, as women, wear. These unique challenges prompted me to reflect annually, evaluating my choices and setting goals for the year ahead.

My Approach

Along with a trusted friend, we dedicated two days each year to deep introspection. Our discussions transcended casual exchanges. We delved into the multifaceted experiences of in leadership, grappling with the balance of personal relationships and business demands. We didn't merely converse; we probed, challenged, and unearthed insights about our intertwined roles.

Perspective

Leading a business isn't just about strategizing and executing. For women, it often intertwines with our roles as mothers, partners, caregivers, and more. This journey is uniquely yours, I bring to the table my experience of navigating this intricate dance. Whether you're facing a business challenge, a relationship hurdle, or the delicate balance of both, I'm here to question, helping you in finding clarity and direction.

My Aspiration

I am ardently committed to elevating the next wave of female leaders, especially those who have faced unique challenges. After our mentorship, my hope is that you'll extend your insights, mentoring others in turn. Additionally, I'm establishing a fund to further support budding female entrepreneurs, aiding them in turning their visions into tangible realities.

Commit to Transformation

Annually, we'll revisit, assess, and fine-tune, ensuring your path remains authentic to your multifaceted roles and aspirations.
